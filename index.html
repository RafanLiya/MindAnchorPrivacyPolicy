MindAnchor Privacy Policy

Effective from: April 5, 2025

MindAnchor is committed to protecting your privacy and helping you stay focused online. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your data when you use the MindAnchor Chrome Extension.

1. Information We Collect

MindAnchor may collect the following types of data:

  • Browsing activity: The extension reads webpage content (title, headings, and text) from the active tab solely for the purpose of distraction classification.
  • Website URLs: We analyze URLs to identify blacklisted or whitelisted domains.
  • User-defined preferences: Any categories, site lists (blacklist/whitelist), or other settings saved by you.

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, emails, or passwords.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Classify websites as focus-friendly or potentially distracting
  • Help you build awareness of your browsing habits
  • Provide non-intrusive reminders to stay focused
  • Improve the functionality of the extension

3. Data Storage and Sharing

All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. We do not store or transmit your data to any external servers.

MindAnchor does not share, sell, or transfer any user data to third parties.

4. Your Consent

By using the MindAnchor Chrome Extension, you consent to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

5. Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
